Our Story

From Civic Frustration
to Platform Infrastructure

NUPA was born from a simple observation: Nigeria holds some of the most consequential elections on the African continent, but the data infrastructure supporting those elections — for campaigns, civil society, and citizens — is dangerously fragmented and underpowered. Pasea Techsolutions, with over a decade of experience in enterprise platforms and civic technology, set out to build the unified electoral intelligence ecosystem Nigeria has always needed.

Mission & Vision

Why NUPA Exists

🏛️
Our Mission
To design, deploy, and democratise political intelligence technology — providing campaigns, civil society organisations, electoral bodies, media, researchers, and citizens with the data infrastructure, analytical tools, and civic engagement platforms needed to participate meaningfully in free, fair, peaceful, and credible elections.
🌍
Our Vision
To be Africa's most trusted, scalable, and impactful election technology ecosystem — transforming democratic participation from a passive right into an active, data-driven, citizen-led force for accountable governance across Nigeria and the African continent.
⚖️
Non-Partisan Mandate
NUPA is not a campaign tool. It is democracy infrastructure. We serve all legitimate stakeholders in Nigeria's electoral ecosystem equally — all parties, all CSOs, all media. This neutrality is our most important asset and most carefully guarded operating principle.
🔬
Theory of Change
Evidence-based democracy is stronger democracy. When campaigns use ward-level intelligence instead of instinct, when CSOs coordinate through shared data instead of silos, when citizens access verified results instead of rumour — elections become harder to manipulate and easier to trust.
Core Values

What We Stand For

01
Data Sovereignty
Nigeria's electoral data belongs to Nigeria. All data is hosted on Nigerian infrastructure, governed by NDPA 2023, and managed under NDPC oversight. No data is exported, commercialised, or shared without explicit lawful basis.
02
Radical Transparency
Annual audited financial reports. Public methodology documentation. Open data APIs. NDPA compliance statements. We hold ourselves to the same transparency standards we demand from the electoral system we monitor.
03
Democratic Neutrality
NUPA serves APC, PDP, LP, ADC, and all other parties with identical access and service quality under NDA-protected data separation. We never advocate for electoral outcomes. Intelligence, not ideology.
04
Local Relevance
Built for Nigeria, not adapted for it. USSD fallback for low-internet LGAs. Offline-capable mobile apps. Content in Hausa, Yoruba, Igbo, and Pidgin. Ward-level granularity that matches how Nigeria is actually governed.
05
Civic Equity
The free citizen tier is not an afterthought. 93M+ registered voters deserve the same access to electoral information that campaigns pay millions for. NUPA's public tools — PVC checker, PU locator, candidate profiles — are always free.
06
Long-Term Thinking
NUPA is built for the 2027 election and every election after it. The governance intelligence products, longitudinal data archive, and West Africa expansion are all investments in democracy infrastructure that outlives any single election cycle.
